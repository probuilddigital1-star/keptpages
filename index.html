Grandma Rose's Apple Pie
From the Rose Family Collection
Ingredients
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup butter, cold
  • 6 Granny Smith apples
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • pinch nutmeg
Instructions
  1. Mix flour and butter until crumbly. Press into pan.
  2. Peel and thinly slice apples. Toss with sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  3. Pile filling high in crust. Dot with butter.
  4. Add top crust and pinch edges to seal. Bake at 375°F for 45 minutes.
Family Note Use tart apples — sweet ones make it too sugary. Mom always said this was Nana's secret.
